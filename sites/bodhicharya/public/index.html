The Foundation

The Bodhicharya Foundation is a legally registered non-profit organization in Nepal, established in 2024 under the Nepal Government's Companies Act of 2006. We are dedicated to promoting inner peace, mindfulness, and compassion through the teachings of Buddhism. Our foundation serves as a platform for individuals seeking spiritual growth, personal transformation, and a deeper connection to themselves and the world around them.
