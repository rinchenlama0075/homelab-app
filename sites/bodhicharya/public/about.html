Bodhicharya Foundation

A platform for individuals seeking spiritual growth, personal transformation, and a deeper connection to themselves and the world around them.

The Foundation

The Bodhicharya Foundation is a legally registered non-profit organization in Nepal, established in 2024 under the Nepal Government's Companies Act of 2006. We are dedicated to promoting inner peace, mindfulness, and compassion through the teachings of Buddhism.

Our foundation serves as a platform for individuals seeking spiritual growth, personal transformation, and a deeper connection to themselves and the world around them.

Our Core

At the core of the Bodhicharya Foundation is a commitment to cultivating love, kindness, and wisdom in all aspects of life. We believe that by nurturing mindfulness and compassion, we can create a more harmonious world where individuals and communities thrive in peace and understanding.

Our mission is to offer resources, guidance, and support to those who wish to embark on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual enlightenment.

Registration

Bodhicharya Foundation is legally registered under the Nepal Government's Companies Act, 2006, pursuant to sub-section (1) of section 5, and was established in 2024.
